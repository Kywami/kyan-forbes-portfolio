Look at These Legendaries!

The Legendary Beasts or Trio of Sacred are a trio of Legendary Pokemon consisting of Raikou, Entei, and Suicune. They were first discovered in the Johto region. They have also been referred to as Beasts of Legend. In Pokemon Super Mystery Dungeon, they are known as Raikou the Lightning, Entei the Volcanic, and Suicune the Gale.

Entei embodies the passion of magma. This Pokemon is thought to have been born in the eruption of a volcano. It sends up massive bursts of fire that utterly consume all that they touch.

Raikou embodies the speed of lightning. The roars of this Pokemon send shock waves shuddering through the air and shake the ground as if lightning bolts had come crashing down.

Suicune embodies the compassion of a pure spring of water. It runs across the land with gracefulness. This Pokemon has the power to purify dirty water.
